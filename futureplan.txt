** Features Old System Has That We DON'T
Absent Hour threshold — punch after a cutoff (e.g. 11 AM) = absent, not just late
Cumulative late penalty — 3 lates in a row → next day auto-absent
Cumulative early penalty — same for early-outs
Night shift processing — cross-midnight punch pairing
Gender-based shift times — separate female hours
Duty roster / work-group scheduling — rotating shifts per group
Working hours + OT calculation — with rounding policies
Breaktime deduction
Inline holiday as absent — sandwiched holiday rule
Early-in / late-out punch windows — punches outside the window ignored

** Features We Have That Old System DOESN'T
Pending status — never falsely marks absent when device was offline
Self-healing reprocess queue — late-arriving data rebuilds automatically
Worked-on-off-day flag — clean tracking of weekend/holiday duty
Shift overrides — single govt-order change applies to everyone
Locked records — manual adjustments protected from reprocessing
Modern, fast architecture — pre-processed, indexed

** Summary
The old system is far more feature-rich in attendance policy (OT, night shifts, cumulative penalties, gender timing, duty rosters, working hours) — but it's slow, complex, and recalculates everything on every report view.

Our new system is simpler and faster, with better data integrity (pending status, self-healing), but it deliberately drops: overtime, working hours, night shifts, cumulative penalties, and the absent-hour threshold.
